Football Legends

Tom Landry was best known as his term as the head coach of the Dallas Cowboys. Landry was the first head coach of the Cowboys serving from 1960 until he was fired by Jerry Jones in 1988. As head coach of the Cowboys, Landry set an NFL record with 20 consecutive winning seasons from 1966-1985. Landry was inducted into the Football Hall of Fame in 1990.

Herb Adderley won five NFL Championships with the Packers during a seven-year span from 1961-67, but he was traded to the Dallas Cowboys in exchange for two players just weeks before the 1970 season.

Troy Aikman was a quarterback for the Dallas Cowboys from 1989 - 2000. While playing for the Cowboys, Aikman led the Cowboys to four consecutive NFL championship games and three Super Bowl victories. Aikman was inducted into the Texas Sports Hall of Fame in 2002.
